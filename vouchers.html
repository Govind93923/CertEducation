Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some questions that might be burning through your mind.

Q1. Do vouchers expire?
Yes, most IT certification vouchers have a strict expiration date, typically ranging from 6 to 12 months from the date of purchase. It is highly recommended to book and take your exam before this date, as extensions are usually not provided by the vendors.
Q2. When is a voucher issued to a person?
A voucher is issued directly to a person via email immediately after successful registration and payment confirmation. Our team ensures speedy delivery so you can schedule your exam without delay.
Q3. How soon can I expect my exam vouchers?
Typically, you can expect to receive your official exam voucher within 24 to 48 hours. If you urgently need the voucher for an immediate booking, please contact us on WhatsApp!
Q4. Can I use a voucher for more than one exam?
No. Every exam voucher contains a unique, single-use code. Once it is applied to schedule an exam, it becomes invalid for any future uses or different exam codes.
Q5. How do I cancel or reschedule my exam?
To cancel or reschedule an exam, you must log in directly to the vendor's official portal (such as Pearson VUE or Prometric). Note that cancellations usually need to happen at least 24-48 hours prior to your scheduled exam time to avoid forfeiting your voucher code.
